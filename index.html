About Me

I'm a third-year Computer Engineering student dedicated to self-reflection, self-discovery, and self-improvement. Currently on leave until Summer 2025, I am focusing on gaining practical experience and enhancing my personal development.

As an AI Trainer, I tested and optimized large language models, improving their performance through prompt engineering techniques like few-shot learning and reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF). This hands-on experience sharpened my problem-solving and critical thinking.

As a STEM Tutor for TRIO Student Support Services, I led over 30 tutoring sessions in calculus and programming. I adapted my teaching with strategies like spaced repetition, active recall, and the Feynman technique while introducing productivity tools like Notion and the Pomodoro Technique to help students manage their time effectively. My work provided marginalized individuals with valuable resources for academic growth.

In my role as President and former Campus Affairs Senator of the Michigan State University chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE), I represented a 50+ member chapter. I enhanced board efficiency by constructing a task-management system and streamlining communication- efforts that facilitated the chapter's receipt of multiple awards (e.g., MSU Outstanding Academic Organization of the Year). Securing 97% support for the presidency, I achieved the first fully-staffed executive board since 2019 by mobilizing student leaders around a unified vision.

During my Controls Engineer internship at Lear Corporation in Dallas, TX, I discovered and resolved a critical false-pass bug in PLC code using process analysis and ladder logic, leading to a company-wide investigation. I prepared reports and presentations for engineers and upper management, analyzed assembly-line camera data with specialized software to recommend seat assembly improvements, and devised a backup contingency plan for PFMEA to ensure continuity in the seat-shipping process for 30+ trailers.

At the KPMG Future of Tech Summit Case Competition in Orlando, FL, I collaborated with a cross-disciplinary team to develop a solution for community carbon neutrality, delivering a compelling presentation that led us to place as finalists.

Looking ahead, I'm seeking out software engineering internships for Summer 2026. Connect with me to discuss shared interests or internship opportunities.
