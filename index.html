About Me

I'm a third-year Computer Engineering student dedicated to self-reflection, self-discovery, and self-improvement. Currently on leave until Summer 2025, I am focusing on gaining practical experience and enhancing my personal development.

As an AI Trainer, I tested and optimized large language models, improving their performance through prompt engineering techniques like few-shot learning and reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF). This hands-on experience sharpened my problem-solving and critical thinking.

As a STEM Tutor for TRIO Student Support Services, I led 30+ tutoring sessions, leveraging knowledge of learning styles and study techniques, including spaced repetition and active recall, to empower students. My focus on time management and problem-solving provided valuable resources for growth to marginalized individuals.

In my role as President and former Campus Affairs Senator of the National Society of Black Engineers at Michigan State University, I represented a chapter of 50+ members. I enhanced board operational efficiency by implementing a task-management system and fostering inter- and intra-chapter collaboration, paving the way for multiple university awards. Securing 97% support for the presidency, I achieved the first fully-staffed executive board since 2019, demonstrating my ability to mobilize student leaders toward common goals.

My internship at Lear Corporation in Dallas, TX, as a Controls Engineer during the summer of 2023 provided hands-on experience in troubleshooting and optimizing PLC code. I resolved a critical bug in a seat functionality test, achieving a 0% false-pass rate. I also completed a PLC-based poke yoke project, presenting my proposal to upper management, showcasing my adaptability and technical communication skills. Utilizing IV2- and IV3-Navigator software, I analyzed data from Keyence Vision Sensors to recommend quality improvements for seat assembly.

Additionally, I participated in the KPMG Future of Tech Summit Case Competition in Orlando, FL, where I collaborated with a cross-disciplinary team to develop a solution promoting carbon neutrality. Leveraging critical thinking and effective communication, I helped deliver a compelling presentation to firm leadership, leading us to place as finalists.

Looking ahead, I'm seeking out software engineering internships for Summer 2026. Connect with me to discuss shared interests or internship opportunities.
